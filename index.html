School Life

these people are pass leaders and SRC members of Cecil Hills.

SRC leaders are elected seniors, (12th grade students) of the school who control and coordinate school events and things that happened around the school. This is a one time thing as at the end of the year, you are farewelled with your fellow mates and set off into the real world.



What is Connect? You may ask.

Well... Connect is an extra class that you take before the first period. From 8:30am-9:00am. This "connect" class is basically considered rollcall. However, its purpose allows students to be aware of school events and other things realting to their school life. Students will learn about more daily life issues and how they are react to these situations. These issues are like, friendship problems, health, responiblity and etc.
